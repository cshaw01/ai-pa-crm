## Quick Lookup Guide

| Question type | Where to look |
|---------------|---------------|
| Who is this person? | `clients/` or `leads/` — match by phone/email/whatsapp |
| Who is overdue / due this week? | `clients/corporate/_INDEX.md` + `clients/residential/_INDEX.md` |
| Who is available to send? | `technicians/_INDEX.md` |
| Do we have this part in stock? | `equipment/_INDEX.md` |
| AC not cooling / water leak / noise | `troubleshooting/_INDEX.md` |
| Quote or pricing | `contracts/_INDEX.md` |
| How to handle emergency / complaint | `sop/_INDEX.md` |
