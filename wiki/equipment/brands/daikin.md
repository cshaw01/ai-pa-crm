# Daikin AC Units

## Overview
Daikin is the most common **residential** AC brand in Malaysia, known for inverter technology and reliability.

## Common Models in Our Service Area

### Residential Split (Inverter)
| Model | Capacity | Typical Use | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------------|-------|
| **FTKM25** | 1.0 HP | Small bedrooms (≤150 sqft) | Most efficient for small rooms |
| **FTKM35** | 1.5 HP | Master bedrooms (150-250 sqft) | **Most common model** |

## Common Issues

### FTKM Split Series
| Issue | Frequency | Likely Cause | Part P/N |
|-------|-----------|--------------|----------|
| Not cooling | Low | Low gas (R32) | R32 refrigerant |
| Error code A6 | Medium | Fan motor failure | Motor P/N varies |
| Error code A3 | Low | Drain pump failure | P/N: S211366 |
| Bad smell | Medium | Dirty filters/evaporator | No part needed (clean) |

## Service Tips
- **R32 refrigerant:** Flammable, requires special handling (CERTAC certification required)
- **Inverter PCBs:** Sensitive to voltage spikes, check power supply first
- **Error codes:** Display on indoor unit LED (flash pattern)
