# Mitsubishi Electric AC Units

## Overview
Mitsubishi Electric is the most common **commercial** AC brand in Malaysia, known for reliability and VRF systems.

## Common Models in Our Service Area

### Cassette Units (Commercial)
| Model | Capacity | Typical Use | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------------|-------|
| **FDT40VGAVX** | 1.5 HP | Offices, retail | Most common in KL offices |
| **FDT32VGAVX** | 1.25 HP | Meeting rooms | Smaller cassette |
| **FDT60VGAVX** | 2.5 HP | Large retail, food courts | High capacity, heavy duty |

## Common Issues by Model

### FDT Cassette Series
| Issue | Frequency | Likely Cause | Part P/N |
|-------|-----------|--------------|----------|
| Water leakage | Medium | Drainage pump failure | PAC-SK24SG |
| Rattling noise | Low | Loose fan motor cover | N/A (tighten screws) |
| Not cooling | Low | Gas leak (rare) | R410A refrigerant |

### PURY-P VRF Series
| Issue | Frequency | Likely Cause | Part P/N |
|-------|-----------|--------------|----------|
| **Water leakage** | **High** | Drain pump fails after 3-4 years | PAC-SK24SG |
| Error code 1102 | Medium | High pressure switch | T-1102-SW |
| Not cooling (one zone) | Low | Zone valve stuck | PAC-VD51KT |

### MSY-GE Residential Series
| Issue | Frequency | Likely Cause | Part P/N |
|-------|-----------|--------------|----------|
| Strange noise (fan) | Medium | Fan motor bearing | P/N: KFR-35 |
| Not cooling | Medium | Low gas (R410A) | R410A refrigerant |
| Error code E04 | Low | Indoor coil sensor | P/N: RT-04 |

## Service Tips
- **Drainage pumps on VRF:** Always check during quarterly service (common failure point)
- **Cassette units:** Require ceiling access panel (bring ladder or access tool)
- **VRF systems:** Error codes displayed on remote controller, not indoor unit
