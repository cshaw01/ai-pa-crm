# Parts: Drainage Pumps

## Overview
Drainage pumps are the #1 emergency part needed, especially for cassette and VRF systems.

## Common Pump Models

### Mitsubishi PAC-SK24SG
**Most common pump in our inventory**

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Voltage** | 220-240V, 50Hz |
| **Flow Rate** | 7 L/min |
| **Max Head** | 8 meters |
| **Dimensions** | 139 x 148 x 105 mm |
| **Compatible With** | FDT40, FDT60, PURY-P series |

**Failure Symptoms:**
- No humming sound when unit runs
- Water in drain pan
- Intermittent leakage before total failure
- Age: Typically fails after 3-5 years

**Installation Notes:**
- Turn OFF power before replacement
- Disconnect 3 wires ( Live, Neutral, Earth)
- 4 drain hose connections (2 in, 1 out, 1 overflow)
- Test with 240V before reassembling
