## SLA Tiers (Included in Contract)

### SLA Tier 1 (Premium)
- **Response Time:** 2 hours (emergency), 24 hours (routine)
- **Penalty:** RM 500/hour if response exceeds SLA
- **Included For:** Menara KL Office Tower (RM 18,000/month)
- **Price Adder:** +20% on base rate

### SLA Tier 2 (Standard)
- **Response Time:** 4 hours (emergency), 48 hours (routine)
- **Penalty:** RM 200/hour if response exceeds SLA
- **Included For:** TechCorp, Pavilion, KL Sentral
- **Price Adder:** +10% on base rate

### SLA Tier 3 (Basic)
- **Response Time:** 6 hours (emergency), 72 hours (routine)
- **Penalty:** RM 100/hour if response exceeds SLA
- **Included For:** Sunway Carnival (quarterly contract)
- **Price Adder:** Base rate only

## Payment Terms
- **Monthly Invoicing:** 1st of each month
- **Payment Terms:** 14-30 days (based on credit approval)
- **Late Payment:** 1.5% per month on outstanding balance
- **Deposit:** 2 months (for new contracts)

## Renewal Terms
- **Automatic Renewal:** Month-to-month after initial term
- **Price Review:** Annual CPI adjustment (typically 3-5%)
- **Termination:** 2 months notice by either party
